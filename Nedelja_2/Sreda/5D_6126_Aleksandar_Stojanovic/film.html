Film: SPEED

Opis:

Hold on tight, because you'll embark on a tense thriller with dizzying stunts and an unexpected romantic story - a movie you'll want to watch over and over again. Keanu Reeves plays Jack Traven, an agent on a Los Angeles special police team sent to defuse a bomb planted on a bus by a psychopathic killer ( Dennis Hopper ). But for his risky venture to succeed, Jack and a passenger ( Sandra Bullock ) must maintain a speed of over 50 miles per hour, or the bomb will explode.
